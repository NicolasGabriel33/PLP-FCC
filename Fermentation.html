Introduction

Welcome to the Home Fermentation guide.

Here we will gonna learn the basics about home fermenting processes. Basically speaking, you'll see how you can ferment anything, from fruits to vegetables, and all you need is a few ingredients, a clean recipient and a bit of care/patience.

This is a simple lacto-fermentation process made with oranges, but you can do with basically any fruit or vegetal.

Simple Fermentation Guide

In the fermentation process the most important thing is: Have all your tools clean.

Here is some things that you'll need:

    Ingredients:

  • 4 organic oranges
  • 1 litre of water.
  • 20g natural salt

    Preparation:

  1. Cut the oranges crosswise.
  2. Put 5g of salt inside each orange.
  3. Place the oranges in a large, clean glass and press them firmly into it.
  4. Pour water over them so that they are completely covered with liquid.
  5. Weight the oranges down to keep them below the brine.
  6. Cover with a tight-fitting lid and ferment at room temperature until desired flavor and texture are achieved. Depending on the temperature, that is from a month to 6 month.
  7. During the first 4 to 8 days you should release excess pressure.
  8. Once the orange is fermented, move to cold storage.

Comment

This is the recipe from the video above. This is a simple guide for lacto-fermented Oranges, one of a few kind of fermentations.

The lacto fermentation is a process where you pour water and salt in some fruits, fruit peels or vegetals to create a anaerobic and salt-brined eviroment where most if not all bad bacterias dies and the good ones like the lacto-bacillus can do its 'job' and create a food that is both safe to consume and is healty.

There is other kind of fermentation processes where you can add suggar instead of salt and make fizzy drinks for an example, it's a whole universe to explore! Do some tests and try different combinations!
